Chebyshev's Inequality

Probability that any X is α times further from μ than σ is ≤ 1 ⁄ α 2 no not negativity

Formulation

∀α ≥ 1 P(|X-μ| ≥ ασ) ≤ 1/ α 2

or (formulation 2)

Easer to prove, use

a = α σ

∀a ≥ σ P(|X-μ| ≥ a) ≤ σ 2 ⁄ a 2
